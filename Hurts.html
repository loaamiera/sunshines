Hurts



You ate your words and now your stomach hurts.
How does it feel to be me?
Victim complexes will never work,
now don’t you just want to flee?
The letters sit at the pit of your stomach,
do you know how much it fills me with glee?
Because you ate your words and your stomach hurts,
and now you know what it’s like to be me.
