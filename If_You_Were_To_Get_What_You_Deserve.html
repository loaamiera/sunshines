If You Were To Get What You Deserve



I wish I never met you
Im tossing and turning
I really hope you are too
Hope your heart is yearning
For at last, me to touch you
I miss the way your smile
Would give me so much faith
And how you'd run for miles
To put the same one on my face
And as I write every line
I happen to be dreaming
Of a world where we exist
And so tonight as I lay
On my pillow of tears
I will go ahead and pray
That I won't let you back in
